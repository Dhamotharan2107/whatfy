1 What Whatfy Is

Whatfy is a third-party WhatsApp automation platform that connects to WhatsApp Web using the same mechanism as WhatsApp Web in your browser. It enables businesses to automate replies, send invoices, manage inventory, and schedule appointment reminders through their WhatsApp account.

⚠️
Not an Official WhatsApp Product

Whatfy is not built, affiliated with, or approved by WhatsApp or Meta Platforms, Inc. WhatsApp does not provide an official API through this tool. This tool uses the WhatsApp Web protocol, which Meta does not officially support for automation. Usage may violate WhatsApp's Terms of Service.

🔍
WhatsApp Does Not Monitor This Tool

WhatsApp/Meta has no direct visibility into or analysis of the Whatfy platform. However, they do monitor message patterns on their network — high volume, spam-like behavior, or mass messaging to non-contacts will trigger their automated detection systems, regardless of which tool you used to send the messages.

2 WhatsApp Ban Risk — What You Must Know

This is the most important section. WhatsApp actively monitors for behavior that looks like spam or abuse. The following actions will result in your number being temporarily restricted or permanently banned:

🚫
Actions That WILL Get You Banned

Sending the same message to hundreds of unknown contacts at once · Messaging people who haven't saved your number · Using your number for marketing to cold contacts · Sending promotional content to people who didn't opt in · Having too many people report your messages as spam · Running hundreds of bulk messages per hour continuously.

When WhatsApp bans a number, the ban is permanent and non-reversible. Whatfy cannot help you recover a banned number. You will lose all your WhatsApp history, contacts, and business presence on that number.

3 Bulk Messaging — Safe Limits

Our platform includes a bulk campaign feature. To protect your number from being banned, you must respect the following recommended safe limits. These are not hard technical limits — they are our recommended thresholds based on observed behavior:

Scenario Safe Limit Risk Level
Messages per hour (warm account, 6+ months old) ≤ 100–150 Low Risk
Messages per hour (new account, < 3 months) ≤ 30–50 Medium Risk
Messages per hour (any account) > 300 Very High — Likely Ban
Sending same message to 1000+ contacts at once Never Instant Ban Risk
Minimum delay between bulk messages 3–10 seconds Recommended
Sending to contacts who haven't saved your number Avoid High Risk
Sending to opted-in customers (invoices, reminders) Safe at normal business volumes Low Risk
⚡
Running Multiple Bulk Campaigns Simultaneously

Sending multiple large bulk campaigns at the same time from the same WhatsApp number dramatically increases ban risk. The simultaneous spike in outgoing messages looks like coordinated spam to WhatsApp's detection systems. Always run one campaign at a time and space them out.
